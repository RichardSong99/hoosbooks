About HoosBooks

The Mission

To empower the pursuit of learning by connecting all students to the exchange of books and of the ideas therein.

The Story

On Friday May 11, 2018, the idea of HoosBooks was born when my friend and Chi Alpha Elder Benjamin Groff, in his final words to me, decried the woeful disarray of the Facebook page on which UVA students were then exchanging textbooks with each other. Sensing that my incomplete yet functional knowledge of computer science could be of use, on the train ride home the selfsame day I made it my mandate to deliver to my fellow Hoos a new marketplace for textbooks erected on two principles: efficiency of searching and completeness of information.

Having completed the project on my study abroad trip in Europe, today I invite all students in the University of Virginia to use HoosBooks. Although this platform is far from perfect, I hope that you will find it to be a significant improvement from the Facebook page and a useful tool in your search for well-priced textbooks every semester. Thank you.

-Richard Song, UVA Class of 2021
